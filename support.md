# Support

Need a hand with **PM Cram (PMP Prep)**? Most questions are answered below. If you're still
stuck, email us and we'll help.

**Contact:** [ForgeDeck@pm.me](mailto:ForgeDeck@pm.me)

We aim to reply within a few business days.

---

## Getting started

PM Cram works entirely on your device — no account or sign-up is needed. Install it, and the
free starter set (glossary cards, practice questions and an exam-tips guide) is ready to use
straight away, online or offline.

## Unlocking the full library

The complete glossary, full question bank, all study guides and every case study — plus the
timed mock exams — are unlocked with a **single one-time purchase** (no subscription). Tap
**Unlock** on the Study tab or in Settings to see the price for your region and buy.

## Restoring a purchase

Already bought the unlock (for example on another device, or after reinstalling)?

1. Open **Settings** (or the Unlock screen) in the app.
2. Tap **Restore purchase**.
3. Make sure you're signed in to the **same Apple ID or Google account** you bought it with.

Your unlock is tied to your store account and restores for free on your other devices.

## "I paid but nothing unlocked"

- Tap **Restore purchase** (above) — this resolves it in almost every case.
- Confirm you're signed in to the store account used for the purchase.
- Check your internet connection and reopen the app.
- Still stuck? Email **[ForgeDeck@pm.me](mailto:ForgeDeck@pm.me)** with the approximate date of
  purchase and your device type, and we'll sort it out.

## Refunds

Purchases are handled by the App Store or Google Play, so refunds are managed there:

- **Apple:** [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
- **Google Play:** [support.google.com/googleplay](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637)

## Study reminders

You can enable optional daily study reminders in the app. These are local notifications on your
device — you can turn them on or off any time in the app or in your device's notification
settings.

## Does it work offline?

Yes. Once content is downloaded, you can study without a connection. New content syncs
automatically when you're back online.

## Found a content issue or have a suggestion?

We're always improving the material. If you spot an error in a question, definition or guide, or
have a feature idea, email **[ForgeDeck@pm.me](mailto:ForgeDeck@pm.me)** — please include the
question/topic so we can find it quickly.
